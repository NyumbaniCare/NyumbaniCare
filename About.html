About NyumbaniCare

NyumbaniCare is a Tanzanian home care solution dedicated to serving individuals who are unable or prefer not to visit hospitals. Our mission is to make quality healthcare accessible and convenient for everyone, right in the comfort of their homes.

Headquarters

We operate from Dar es Salaam, with our primary care services anchored at the renowned Muhimbili National Hospital in Upanga West, Ilala District. This institution stands as Tanzania’s national referral hospital and a leading academic and research hub in the region.

Our Services

  • Home-Based Medical Care: Personalized care delivered at home.
  • Nursing Services: Professional nursing support tailored to individual needs.
  • Physiotherapy: Rehabilitative services to aid mobility and recovery.
  • Palliative Care: Comfort and support for those with serious health conditions.
  • Maternal and Neonatal Care: Specialized attention for mothers and newborns.
  • Geriatric Care: Comprehensive care for the elderly.

Why Choose Us?

  • Professional Staff: Qualified experts dedicated to quality care.
  • Convenience: We deliver care right to your door.
  • Comprehensive Care: From check-ups to specialized services.
